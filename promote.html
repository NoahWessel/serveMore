PROMOTE

As a morehead citizen, promoting your needs has never been easier. By selecting the button below, you will be taken to a simple form that will allow you to lay out your opportunity in a concise and organized fashion. You will provide info such as a description, goals hoping to achieve, and pictures. As students search available opportunities and land on yours, they will apply with an application, you will contact them, and then it's time to get to work!

Every time you post an opportunity for Morehead students to help you, you are increasing their ability to grow and succeed in the community and in life. Classroom education is great, but there is nothing like hands on experience. ServeMore makes these personal and relevant experiences possible, and it starts with your intitiative. Promote an opportunity today and take a step towards a better tomorrow!
